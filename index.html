About Us

At Basu Pens, we believe that every great idea begins with a simple stroke of a pen. Writing is more than just a daily activity—it is a powerful way to express thoughts, creativity, and knowledge. With this belief, Basu Pens is dedicated to providing high-quality writing instruments that make every word smooth, clear, and meaningful. Our journey started with a vision to create pens that combine comfort, durability, and modern design. We understand that a good pen should not only write well but also feel right in your hand. That is why each Basu pen is crafted with attention to detail, ensuring a consistent ink flow, long-lasting performance, and a comfortable grip for everyday use. At Basu Pens, we focus on meeting the needs of students, professionals, and writers who rely on pens in their daily lives. Whether it’s taking notes in class, signing important documents, or expressing creative ideas, our pens are designed to deliver reliability and precision every time. Quality and affordability are at the heart of everything we do. We aim to provide products that are accessible to everyone without compromising on performance or style. Our commitment to customer satisfaction drives us to continuously improve and innovate our designs. We also believe that a pen is not just a tool—it is a companion in your journey of learning, working, and creating. Every line you write tells a story, and Basu Pens is proud to be a part of those stories.

Our Eco mission

The future of our planet is being written today and every choice we make leaves a mark.

At BASU PENS, sustainability is at the core of everything we do. We believe that innovation should not come at the cost of nature, which is why we focus on using alternative and responsible materials in our products. Wherever possible, we encourage the reuse of waste materials, including those generated from agricultural sources, turning what is often discarded into something useful and valuable. This approach helps reduce unnecessary waste while giving new life to resources that would otherwise harm the environment. Our processes are designed with care to minimize pollution and reduce environmental impact at every stage, from material selection to production. Instead of contributing to the growing problem, we aim to be part of a smarter and cleaner solution. By combining thoughtful design with environmental responsibility, we strive to create products that support both quality and sustainability without compromising the health of our planet.

How We Make Difference

The Environmental Problem

Our planet is facing a growing crisis due to plastic waste and pollution. Millions of disposable pens are thrown away every day, contributing to environmental damage that affects land, water, and air. This pollution disturbs ecosystems, harms wildlife, and threatens biodiversity, creating an urgent need for responsible choices.

Our Mission To Solve The Problem

At BASU PENS, our mission is to reduce environmental impact by promoting eco-conscious writing solutions. We aim to minimize waste, encourage sustainability, and protect the natural balance of our planet by designing products that are both practical and responsible.

Why To Choose Us?

Because choosing BASU PENS means choosing responsibility. Our products are designed with care for the environment, helping you reduce waste while supporting a cleaner and healthier ecosystem. Every purchase you make becomes a step toward protecting biodiversity and building a sustainable future.

What We Do?

We create high-quality pens using thoughtful design and responsible materials that focus on durability and reduced environmental impact. Our goal is to provide writing tools that not only perform well but also contribute to a greener planet.

Together, we can write a better future — one pen at a time.

Our Founder

Idea Studio 4 Iditiours

Mr. Dwarika Prashad Joshi

Founder & CEO

Basu Pens was founded by Dwarika Prashad Joshi, with a vision to build a strong and self-reliant stationery manufacturing brand in Nepal. His goal was to create a company that represents quality, innovation, and local production, while reducing dependency on imported writing products. He aimed to contribute to the growth of Nepal’s industrial sector through meaningful and practical production.

Under his leadership, the company was developed with the idea of producing reliable and affordable pens that meet everyday needs. The focus has always been on building a product that balances performance, durability, and accessibility for students, professionals, and general users. Continuous improvement and attention to detail remain key priorities in the production process.

Dwarika Prashad Joshi’s approach to business is centered on long-term thinking, disciplined execution, and strong ethical values. He believes that a brand grows not only through products, but also through trust, consistency, and responsibility toward customers. His leadership style emphasizes quality over quantity and steady progress over shortcuts.

From a simple concept to a growing manufacturing initiative, his journey reflects determination and resilience. Through continuous effort, the idea was transformed into a functioning brand that is gradually gaining recognition in the Nepali market. Each stage of development has been guided by learning, adaptation, and commitment to improvement.

Looking forward, his vision is to expand the brand’s reach and strengthen local manufacturing in Nepal. The long-term goal is to build a sustainable and competitive stationery industry that can stand proudly in both national and future international markets. He also aims to create more opportunities for local employment and innovation.
